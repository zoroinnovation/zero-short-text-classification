# SST-2 Few-Shot Prompt File (with Edge Cases)


Task: Classify the sentiment of a movie review as either Positive or Negative.  
- Handle sarcasm, mixed reviews, and very short reviews carefully.  
- If both pros and cons are present, base the decision on the overall tone.  
- Keep the label strictly "Positive" or "Negative".  

=====================
Few-Shot Examples
=====================

Review: "The movie was an absolute delight, full of charm and great performances."
Sentiment: Positive

Review: "This was a boring and predictable film with no originality." 
Sentiment: Negative

Review: "Loved it!"
Sentiment: Positive

Review: "Oh great, another masterpiece of boredom."
Sentiment: Negative

Review: "The visuals were stunning, but the story made no sense and ruined the experience."
Sentiment: Negative

Review: "A charming movie with heartfelt performances."
Sentiment: Positive

Review: "At least the popcorn was better than the movie."
Sentiment: Negative
